About

Army Veteran • Cybersecurity Specialist • Lifelong Learner

My path into cybersecurity started in an unexpected place—a restaurant. I spent ten years in the service industry, where I not only waited tables but also built and managed the restaurant’s online presence. I learned how to register a domain, configure DNS records, and host a website, making sure the site stayed up and the menu was always accessible. That hands-on curiosity became the foundation for my tech career.

Alongside that, I served nine years in the U.S. Army Reserves, where I learned structure, teamwork, and problem-solving under pressure. Those values now shape how I approach cybersecurity: proactive, organized, and mission-driven.

Since 2021, I’ve worked in IT and cybersecurity, earning my Bachelor’s in Cybersecurity and Information Assurance and gaining experience in infrastructure, endpoint management, and email/network security. I love bridging the gap between systems and people—helping technology run securely, efficiently, and with purpose.
